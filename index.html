Food Delivery Apps in Mexico: Where can it work?

A description of the problem and a discussion of the background


Assumptions:


Imagine that in México, they has not existed any delivery food service like UberEATS, Postmates or Rappi yet

You are part of a Company of food delivery service. In specific, you are in the strategical area

The company took the decision of enter to the Mexican market

So you need to know where is profitable the delivery service


How can we solve the problem?


First, we can determinate which cities and postal codes have the major density of population and restaurants, the variety of restaurants that a zone have and the closeness with another postal code (this is important to know because the restaurants or commensals of a neighbor postal code maybe require the service).

Second, with this information we can make a model to determinate the potential demand of the food delivery service in the cities and recommended where the company can implement the service.

A description of the data and how it will be used to solve the problem.


For make this analysis we need data about:


Which cities and Postal codes have the majority density of population





How many restaurants and the variety of types of restaurant have a specific postal code





Parameters about the behavior of the consumers that the company observed in other counties. For example, the percentage of people and restaurants that used the delivery service and the frequency


Data sources:


CYBO is a web site that utilized tools and data from other sites like Google maps, Wikipedia, national census etc. In this pages we can obtained a list of postal codes with de population and area.



Foursquare is a technology company that built a massive dataset of location data. Using the Foursquare API, we can search for specific type of venues or stores around a given location. So, in this data base we can query the restaurants for a specific postal code.
